RetroSims v 1.4

RetroSims is an unofficial remake of EA's game ¨The Sims¨,
giving it a retro aesthetic and
simplifying the original mechanics.

• Customize your player.

• Meet your neighbours.

• Keep an eye on your needs, improve your stats and make some friends.





RetroSims is a game by: David Carmona Fauste and Mario Tabasco Vargas
